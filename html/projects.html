Natura Cosmetics

Brazil

Natura Cosmetics is one of the largest cosmetics retailers in Brazil and in 2017, they launched their Carbon Neutral Programme. It has reduced gas emissions and in turn eliminated air travel when not necessary. All plastic that is used by the company is recycled plastic.

“Natura Logo.” Natura Cosmetics, 2022, https://www.naturabrasil.com/. Accessed 10 Jan. 2022.

Climate Change Champions Project

California, United States

Through Climate Change Champions, health professionals in California receive training on how to advise healthcare on the effects of climate change on health. These health professionals can then take part in media advocacy and policy advisory.

“Climate Change Champions.” CPM Magazine, 2022, https://www.cpm-magazine.co.uk/. Accessed 10 Jan. 2022.

CRAFT

Multiple Countries

Generates funds to be the first private sector fund investment fund for developing technologies to combat climate change and adaptation in developing countries. The technology reaches these countries through a mechanical system. The technology includes water harvesting, irrigation systems, and geospatial mapping.

“CRAFT.” Green Climate, 2021, https://www.greenclimate.fund/project/fp181. Accessed 10 Jan. 2022.

Paris Agreement

Multiple Countries

The Paris Agreement is a worldwide treaty between 196 countries to reduce greenhouse gas emissions to keep global warming from reaching 2 degrees celsius. By mid-century, countries are supposed to peak global emissions to prevent any more global warming. Every five years, countries will submit what they have done to reach their goal through nationally determined contributions or NDCs. The Paris Agreement builds a financial framework to help all the countries a part of the treaty help each other.

“United Nations Climate Change.” UNFCCC, 2022, https://unfccc.int/process-and-meetings/the-paris-agreement/the-paris-agreement. Accessed 10 Jan. 2022.
