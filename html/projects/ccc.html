The United Nations's approach to climate change

  Created by the Center for Climate Change and Health in California, the Climate Change Champions Project is an initiative to help Californian health professionals be better prepared to understand the effects of climate change on human health and how to utilize this information. The Center has partnered with various Californian climate change initiatives to help public health officials be a better voice on trying to reduce our carbon footprint. This project offers leadership and information courses for these professionals to learn what they can do to not only advocate for change but also learn solutions for their communities. Through this project, these health professionals will then be able to engage in all kinds of media to advocate for climate change legislation.
